### 1. 🎙️ THE OBSESSED FOUNDER — Eric's Raw Journey

```

into the psychology of obsessive creation. This should feel like two people
genuinely amazed by what one human can do.
```

### 9. 🎙️ THE MATH EPISODE — CSL Gates for Normal People

```

about B?", CONSENSUS is "what does the group think?", XOR is "what's
unique here?", and ANALOGY is "king minus man plus woman equals queen."
Explain the Fibonacci-derived thresholds: 0.691 for everyday decisions,
0.809 for research, 0.882 for critical security. Make the audience go
"oh, that actually makes sense!" by the end.
```

## Usage

1. Open **Heady Ecosystem** notebook in [NotebookLM](https://notebooklm.google.com/)
2. Click **Audio Overview** in the Studio panel (right side)
3. Click **Customize** and paste any prompt above
4. Click **Generate** — each produces a ~10-15 minute podcast-style conversation
5. The two AI hosts will discuss the topic naturally based on your sources

### Recommended Listening Order
**Eric's Story Arc:** 1 → 3 → 4 → 2
**Technical Deep Dive:** 9 → 6 → 5 → 7
**Business & Vision:** 8 → 11 → 10 → 12
